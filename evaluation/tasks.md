# Retrieval tasks (template)

Each task: provide (1) rationale/tradeoff, (2) supporting artifact ref(s) and relationship type when evidence exists. If a decision is still proposed and has no links, identify the missing evidence instead.

## Task set (minimum 6)
T1: DR-0002 — Why was the Transformer baseline chosen? Provide 1 supporting artifact.
T2: DR-0003 — What changed in the evaluation protocol and why? Provide evaluated_by artifact.
T3: DR-0004 — Why introduce dataset version pinning? Provide supporting document artifact.
T4: DR-0005 — What decision superseded DR-0004? Provide supersedes edge and rationale.
T5: DR-0006 — Why is the metric threshold change still proposed? Explain what evidence is missing.
T6: DR-0001 — What was the initial motivation for decision tracking in this repo? Provide supporting doc.

Time limit: 5 minutes per task.
